GUN MASHER is a top down roguelite shooter with a unique gun-combining system!

Check it out! It's free!

I'm pretty happy with how Cowboy Hell turned out, especially for one of my first projects.

It took me almost exactly one year to make, starting junior year of high school. GitHub says I wrote over 13,000 lines of code!

This game is around 2 hours long and really fills the time with content, there are a lot of crackpot ideas in this. It is a really challenging game and many players repeat sections they die in, or stay stuck in some puzzles.

Cowboy Hell started as a weird inside joke among me and my friends. I think the original line that started the whole thing was "Welcome to Cowboy Hell, where the cowboys are cowboys and the grass is... red" I decided to make a game out of that lol.

There are a lot of inside jokes in the game. All the voices for the various characters are done by my friends. Some of the writing is done by them too. The amazing cover art was done by my friend Cam Chen! I'll have to give a better shout out to him somewhere on this website. He really did an amazing job.

If you find Millie in the game, you're a top echelon Cowboy Hell player, not many people have done that. If you find Kevin mode... O_O

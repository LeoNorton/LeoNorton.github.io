GUN MASHER is a top down roguelite shooter with a unique gun-combining system!

Adventure your way through the procedurally generated steampunk, magic, and overgrown levels, collecting gun pieces as you go!

Gun handles and barrels can be found and combined to form new guns or shot on their own!

Check it out! It's free!

My main focus when making this game was the customizable gun mechanic. Each gun combo is completely programmed by hand, making the game more balanced and more novel than the traditional, procedurally generated gun customization game.

As you find new combos, a 5x5 table of each combo is filled out in the home menu, thats right, there are 25 different guns, or gun combos in this game!

There is also a crazy boss at the end. I like this game even though it wasn't very popular. I will admit, the pace is a little slow, and sometimes you get stuck with really difficult-to-play situations.

CONTROLS

W A S D to move!

Mouse to look around and click to fire!

Q to drop a gun piece!

Shift to dash! (dash through bullets)

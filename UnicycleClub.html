Unicycle Club!

Updated 12/24/2024

I joined Neunicyclers at the start of my time in Boston, or spring of 2023, and am now officially vice president! It's an amazing club and I'm happy to be running it while our president has been gone this year! We just put out a big pile of like, 11 or 12 unicycles every Saturday and anyone is welcome to try to learn or improve their skills or just hang out! We also do some juggling!

The tall unicycle is called the giraffe, I'm one of the only people who rides it. I think the other people could, but are just afraid of falling.

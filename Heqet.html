Heqet is an underwater terror/puzzle game with lots of scary fish!
I currently manage a team of four other amazing Northeastern game developers working with me on Heqet!

Thank you to Devin, Tyler, Eli, and Adam, my amazing team members! We are getting great work done on the game right now.

Heqet is a game with a somewhat avant-garde design philosophy which encourages players to figure out how the underwater world around them works on their own.

Using your powers of telekinesis and telepathy, find out how to navigate the obstacles and creatures of the ocean through open-ended exploration and experimentation!

Throughout the design of Heqet, I've been taking huge inspiration from my favorite game, Outer Wilds. Heqet works as a puzzle game in a loose sense, in that you surpass obstacles by understanding how the world around you works and making inferences. Through exploration, you are able to learn more about how different elements of the world function.

It's knowledge based game progression!

The ZIMBER

Updated 3/1/2025

Here's a nice little lighting system I made to take to Burning Man 2024. It was created for my swing bike, which is an interesting type of custom bike modification which involves adding an extra handlebar joint beneath the bike seat. This allows the rider to ride with the two wheels out of line, such as one on the street and one on the sidewalk.

The glowy lighting system looks like the bike is covered in blinking eyes!

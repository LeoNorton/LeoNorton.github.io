Candy Loser is a creepy, musical, halloween-themed adventure game which takes the player on a trip through a neighborhood in pursuit of candy. This project was a submission for the one week Queen's University Game Jam!

The people, music, and textures in this project were created by Eli Silverstein!

The buildings were modeled by Kaan Volkan!

In this game, you go from house to house and trick or treat. Each house has unique animations and music as a story progresses. I think it's a pretty novel experience. Candy Loser won second place in the jam and we won a like, $45 prize or something.

Candy Loser is a creepy, musical, halloween-themed adventure game which takes the player on a trip through a neighborhood in pursuit of candy. This project was a submission for the one week Queen's University Game Jam!

The people, music, and textures in this project were created by Eli Silverstein!

The buildings were modeled by Kaan Volkan!

In this game, you go from house to house and trick or treat. Each house has unique animations and music as a story progresses. It's a pretty novel experience Candy Loser won second place in the jam and we won a like, $45 prize or something.

Cowboy Hell started as a weird inside joke among me and my friends. I think the original line that started the whole thing was "Welcome to Cowboy Hell, where the cowboys are cowboys and the grass is... red" I decided to make a game out of that lol.

There are a lot of inside jokes in the game. All the voices for the various characters are done by my friends. Some of the writing is done by them too. The amazing cover art was done by my friend Cam Chen! I'll have to give a better shout out to him somewhere on this website. He really did an amazing job.

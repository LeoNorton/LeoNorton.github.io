Burning Man Gaming Console

I plan to give away these little Burning Man themed gaming consoles at this year's burn! Here's a demo of it playing a version of frogger that I burn-themed.

I custom printed this PCB, and the hardware to make these cost me about $4 a piece! That includes the ATtiny microcontroller and the LCD(not LED) screen!

You can't hear it in this video, but the SMD buzzer also plays sounds while you game! (features a mute switch for your convenience)

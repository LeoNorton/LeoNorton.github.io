Updated 3/3/2025

Hooked on Humans was our submission for the GMTK 2023 Game Jam. We made it in maybe two days. It sticks to the theme of the jam, role reversal in that it's a fish "fishing" for people. The game is a beautiful, high octane journey to fish procedurally generated humans while listening to a really cool song.

The most amazing part of this game is the great musical theme. It was recorded by my friends Spencer and Jackson, make sure to sound-on. Each human in this game was procedurally generated, including their dialogue when they're caught. Give it a play!
